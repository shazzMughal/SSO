This is an official webpage of a society of Namal University Mianwali, Whose president is Mr Javad Ahmad.

The OSS(Open Source Society) is a society of Namal University Mianwali. It is funded by the Namal Institute. The Goals of this society is to make the students aware about the emerging fields of technology. The society wants to make the students of namal institute aware about the real industry of Pakistan. The main focus of our society is the "Linux Kernal". We can say that our society is Linux Lover. Our society has conducted several events in the universty. And for this purpose, we have invited the Experienced and Currently Working People from the industry. To Check the events Conducted by the society Click the Learn More Button given below.
